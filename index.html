About Me

Hello, I’m William Parkhouse – an AI and Computer Science student with a passion for building scalable, real-world solutions. At Hewlett Packard Enterprise, I develop cloud-native backend microservices with a focus on clean architecture and rigorous testing. My work includes designing anomaly detection systems with mathematically proven accuracy, implementing CO₂ tracking for microservice emissions, and driving high-throughput data ingestion using technologies like Go, Python, Docker, Kubernetes, Kafka, Prometheus, and Grafana.

My journey into tech began with a passion for music and digital signal processing, which inspired me to create my first VST plugin and explore Music Information Retrieval projects. This blend of creative problem-solving and technical rigor has guided my evolution from audio engineering to co-founding raiss.ai – an AI-driven shopping search platform that leverages RAG agents, ETL pipelines, and advanced NLP to deliver personalized product recommendations. I thrive on solving challenging problems, whether through backend engineering, AI-driven innovation, or full-stack development, and I’m always eager to explore new ways to make a tangible impact.

Feel free to explore my portfolio below or visit raiss.ai to learn more about my startup.
